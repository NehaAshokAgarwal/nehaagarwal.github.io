VOLUNTEERING EXPERIENCE

Hope for Boston's Homelessness

I volunteered with Elaine at Hope for Boston's homelessness from 2021 to 2024. I helped to pack 50-60 backpacks and to distribute them to the people out there in need on alternate weekends. The backpacks contain healthy food items, clothing, sanitary products, and bathing accessories. We recieved these items as donations from our friends, neighbours, and family However, the organization is now called Boston Backpack. If anyone is insterested to join the community, please reach out to Elain through her facebook account .
